About Word Counter

The Word Counter tool provides comprehensive text statistics to help you analyze your content. Perfect for writers, students, bloggers, and anyone who needs to meet specific word count requirements.

Features:

  • Word Count: Total number of words in your text
  • Character Count: Total characters including spaces
  • Character Count (no spaces): Total characters excluding spaces
  • Sentence Count: Number of sentences based on punctuation
  • Paragraph Count: Number of paragraphs separated by line breaks
  • Reading Time: Estimated time to read (based on 200 words/minute)
  • Speaking Time: Estimated time to speak (based on 150 words/minute)
  • Average Word Length: Average number of characters per word

Use Cases:

  • Check essay or article word counts
  • Optimize content for SEO requirements
  • Prepare speeches with time estimates
  • Monitor social media post lengths
  • Analyze text complexity and readability
