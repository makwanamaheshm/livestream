About Case Converter

The Case Converter tool allows you to quickly transform your text between different letter cases. Whether you need to convert to uppercase, lowercase, title case, or programming-specific formats like camelCase or snake_case, this tool makes it simple and fast.

Features:

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capital letters
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • camelCase: First word lowercase, subsequent words capitalized, no spaces
  • PascalCase: All words capitalized, no spaces
  • snake_case: All lowercase with underscores between words
  • kebab-case: All lowercase with hyphens between words
  • Reverse Text: Reverses the order of characters
